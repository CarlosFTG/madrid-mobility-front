Welcome to Madrid Mobility

This app has been developed in order to provide data from the EMT API-REST service

and show it a map, using some GIS functions.

Before continue, I have to tell you that the town where you are located is registered just for statistical reasons, and please, if you agree, allow geolocation in your browser when required.

Bienvenido/a a Madrid Mobility
